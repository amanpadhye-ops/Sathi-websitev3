Our Mission

What drives everything we do

Create safe and protective environments for children in vulnerable circumstances and protect them from neglect, abuse, and exploitation.

Reintegrate runaway, separated, and street-connected children with their families and educational institutions wherever safe and appropriate.

Strengthen the implementation of child protection systems for at-risk children through the involvement of communities, civil society, and state actors.

Document best practices and share learning on the protection, rehabilitation, and reintegration of children into families and communities.

"To ensure that every child lives in a safe, secure, and nurturing environment, with the belief that family is the best place for a child to grow with self-confidence, self-worth, and dignity."
